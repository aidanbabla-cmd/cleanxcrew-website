Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

CleanXCrew ("we", "us", or "our") operates the website cleanxcrew.com. This page explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

When you submit a quote request, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your address
  • Your phone number
  • Your email address (optional)
  • The service(s) you're interested in

We also collect anonymous usage data through Meta Pixel (Facebook) to measure how our ads perform.

How We Use Your Information

  • To contact you with a quote for the services you requested
  • To respond to your questions or follow up on your request
  • To improve our advertising (via Meta Pixel analytics only)

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties for marketing purposes.

Meta Pixel (Facebook)

Our website uses the Meta Pixel to track page visits and form submissions for advertising purposes. This helps us understand how our Facebook ads perform. Meta may use this data in accordance with their own Privacy Policy.

Data Storage

Quote requests are received via Formspree and delivered to our email. We do not operate a database. Your information is retained only as long as needed to respond to your inquiry.

Your Rights

You may request that we delete any personal information you submitted by emailing us. We will respond within 5 business days.

Cookies

We do not use cookies directly. However, Meta Pixel may set cookies on your device. You can opt out of Facebook ad tracking at facebook.com/settings.
